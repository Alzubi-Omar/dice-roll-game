Game Rules

  • Rolling: Each player rolls the dice and accumulates points. If a 1 is rolled, the player loses their current score and their turn ends.
  • Holding: A player can choose to "hold" to add their current score to their total. Then it's the next player's turn.
  • Winning: The first player to reach 100 points wins the game.
  • New Game: Reset scores and start over with Player 1.
